Hemmam Application

</Title>
<Description>

Hemam is an application that helps people with special needs to get the medical help they need as fast and easy as possible.
The user can surf the medical departments, choose which doctor they would like, and book their appointment.
The application offers a “chat with the doctor” feature as well as video and audio calls, which makes communication between the user and the doctor much easier.

Etiq AI's Dashboard

</Title>
<Description>

As a UX/UI Designer at Etiq AI, I was asked to re-design the dashboard of the website.
I re-designed the dashboard and developed 2-3 prototypes per week to improve user experience scores and data readability.
I conducted and created several use cases on over 5,000 records to implement visualizations of data into the dashboard.
I tested and analyzed more than 3 systems to compare with our current one, and I presented my findings in a clear and concise manner to key decision-makers.

<Title>

Exchange

</Title>
<Description>

Exchange is an application where college students can exchange their textbooks for money.
As college textbooks are expensive and most students will not use them after they finish the course, Exchange makes it easy to hand down textbooks to students who cannot afford to pay the full price or are looking to exchange their textbooks for money.
